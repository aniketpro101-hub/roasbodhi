How much does a website cost in India? If you ask five different developers, you will receive five different quotes ranging from ₹5,000 to ₹1,50,000. For small and medium business owners in India, this price variation is confusing. Why does one person charge the price of a mobile phone while another charges the price of a car?

In this guide, we break down website development costs in India in 2025, explaining what you pay for and how to avoid overpaying.

## 1. What Determines Website Cost?

The cost of a website is determined by three main elements:
1.  **Complexity & Page Count:** A simple 3-page landing page is cheaper than a 100-product e-commerce store.
2.  **Tech Stack:** Platforms like WordPress, Wix, or Shopify are faster to build but carry monthly fees. Custom code frameworks like Astro or React offer better speed but require professional development.
3.  **Design Customization:** Using a pre-built template is cheap, but custom UI design built for your brand requires design resources.

---

## 2. Typical Pricing Benchmarks in India (2025)

Here is a breakdown of what you can expect to pay based on who you hire:

### Tier 1: DIY Builders (Wix, Shopify, Squarespace)
*   **Cost:** ₹5,000 to ₹15,000/year (recurring subscription fees).
*   **Pros:** You control the edits.
*   **Cons:** You must invest your own time, and templates often load slowly without speed optimization.

### Tier 2: Local Freelancers
*   **Cost:** ₹5,000 to ₹15,000 (one-time).
*   **Pros:** Very affordable.
*   **Cons:** Post-launch support can be unreliable, and code quality varies. Freelancers may use bloated themes that load slowly.

### Tier 3: Professional Agencies (Like RoasBodhi)
*   **Cost:** ₹10,000 to ₹40,000 (one-time).
*   **Pros:** Custom high-speed development, built-in local SEO schemas, secure hosting support, and reliable project timelines.
*   **Cons:** Requires a larger upfront investment.

---

## 3. Hidden Costs to Watch Out For

*   **Domain & Hosting Renewal:** Domains (.in or .com) cost ₹600 to ₹1,000/year. Premium hosting ranges from ₹3,000 to ₹8,000/year.
*   **Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC):** Many agencies charge a mandatory fee (often 20% of the build cost) to keep the site updated. Ensure you clarify whether AMC is optional.
*   **Content & Copywriting:** Most developers expect you to write your own copy. Hiring a professional copywriter can add ₹5,000 to ₹10,000 to the project.
