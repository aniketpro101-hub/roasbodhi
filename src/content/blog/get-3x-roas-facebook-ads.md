Achieving a high Return on Ad Spend (ROAS) on Facebook and Instagram is the ultimate goal of every performance marketer in India. However, with rising ad competition, changing privacy regulations, and shifting consumer behaviors, achieving a sustainable 3X ROAS is harder than ever. Many business owners launch a campaign, spend thousands of rupees, and end up with no actual sales.

At RoasBodhi™, we have managed ad accounts across real estate, retail, and clinic niches, consistently hitting 3X to 5X benchmarks. In this guide, we share our exact campaign playbook.

## 1. Implement Conversions API (CAPI)

Relying solely on the standard browser-based Meta Pixel is no longer sufficient. Browser extensions, ad-blockers, and iOS tracking restrictions block up to 30% of user data events.

*   **The Solution:** Set up Server-Side tracking via Meta Conversions API (CAPI). This routes user events directly from your server (like your fast Astro website) to Meta's servers.
*   **The Result:** Accurate event attribution, improved matching scores, and lower overall Cost-Per-Lead (CPL).

---

## 2. Leverage Broad Targeting & Advantage+ Campaigns

One of the most common mistakes Indian advertisers make is over-restricting their audiences. Restricting targets to small interests (like "people interested in jewellery + who read Vogue") increases CPM costs.

*   **The Playbook:** Meta's AI is smarter than manual filters. Trust the algorithm. Use broad targeting (targeting only location and age range) or launch Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns. Allow the creatives themselves to filter your audience.

---

## 3. Visual Creative Quality is 80% of the Battle

In 2025, your ad creative is your primary targeting tool. If your video or image is boring, users scroll past it instantly.

### Winning Creative Formulas for India:
1.  **User-Generated Content (UGC):** Selfie videos of customers reviewing your store, jewelry, or clinic appointments. Authentic reviews build trust.
2.  **The Hook in First 3 Seconds:** You must grab attention immediately. Avoid slow logo animations at the start. Lead with the core benefit or a shocking statistic.
3.  **Regional Context:** Speaking to customers in regional dialects or utilizing local greetings (like "Namaste" or "Pranam") significantly lowers CPC costs.

---

## 4. Design High-Speed Landing Pages

You can have the best ad creative in the world, but if your landing page takes 6 seconds to load, 50% of your paid traffic will bounce before the pixel can even load.

*   **Action Step:** Ensure your landing page uses clean framework code (like Astro or Vite) and hits a mobile PageSpeed score above 90. Every second saved reduces drop-off rates, raising your conversion rate.
