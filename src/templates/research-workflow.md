# Bodhi Drishti — Step-by-Step Research Workflow SOP

This document defines the standard operating procedure (SOP) for delivering our research reports from onboarding to post-delivery follow-ups.

---

## 1. PRE-RESEARCH (Day 1)
- [ ] **Onboarding call**: Schedule a 30-60 minute kick-off video call with the client.
- [ ] **Intake questionnaire**: Guide the client through [client-intake-form.md](file:///c:/Users/Aniket/Documents/RoasBodhi/src/templates/client-intake-form.md) and record all answers in their client folder.
- [ ] **Collect links**: Verify website URLs, landing pages, and social media handles of the client.
- [ ] **Lock competitor profiles**: Confirm the list of 3 (Chakshu), 5 (Divya Drishti), or 10 (Trikaldarshi) competitors to analyze.
- [ ] **Establish timeline**: Inform the client of their expected delivery date based on the package:
  - *Chakshu*: 5 business days
  - *Divya Drishti*: 10 business days
  - *Trikaldarshi*: 15 business days

---

## 2. DATA COLLECTION (Days 2-3)
- [ ] **SEMrush Analysis**:
  - Run domain checks for the client and each competitor.
  - Export organic search positions, domain authority (DA) scores, and backlink counts.
  - Harvest organic and paid keyword search profiles.
- [ ] **Ubersuggest Checks**:
  - Cross-verify keyword search volumes, average CPCs, and SEO difficulty indices.
- [ ] **Meta Ad Library audit**:
  - Search competitor names in the Meta Ad Library.
  - Note active ads, hook variations, discount schemes, and landing pages.
  - Screenshot key creative styles and save them to the client's asset folder.
- [ ] **Google Ads Transparency audit**:
  - Check the Google Ads Transparency Center for active Search, Video, and Display ads run by competitors.
- [ ] **Performance audits**:
  - Run Google PageSpeed Insights tests for mobile and desktop for all domains.
  - Export Core Web Vitals performance scores (LCP, FID, CLS).
- [ ] **Social media audit**:
  - Audit competitor Instagram grids, YouTube channel statistics, and Facebook page activity.
  - Use Social Blade to verify follower shifts and engagement patterns.
- [ ] **Google Maps presence (GBP) check**:
  - Search target service keywords locally.
  - Note map rankings, review numbers, rating distributions, and GBP optimizations.
- [ ] **Client review analysis**:
  - Read positive and negative Google Maps reviews for competitors to discover customer pain points.
- [ ] **Google Trends mapping**:
  - Analyze search interest shifts over the last 12-36 months in the target state/city.
- [ ] **Anchor content research**:
  - Search AnswerThePublic for high-intent question topics in the client's industry.

---

## 3. AI STRATEGY & ANALYSIS (Days 3-5)
- [ ] **Consolidate inputs**: Collect all exports, keyword lists, speed reports, and screenshots into a single structured text file.
- [ ] **Choose prompt level**: Open [research-prompts.md](file:///c:/Users/Aniket/Documents/RoasBodhi/src/templates/research-prompts.md) and copy the master prompt matching the client's package level.
- [ ] **Submit to AI**: Paste the populated prompt and harvested data into the LLM system.
- [ ] **Verify outputs**:
  - Fact-check all data points, keyword list CPCs, and competitor names.
  - Validate local Indian cultural and geographic contexts (e.g. check local city naming conventions).
  - Check for placeholders or generic responses and instruct the LLM to write them out in full if found.

---

## 4. REPORT COMPILATION & FORMATTING (Days 5-7)
- [ ] **Canva templates**: Open the master Bodhi Drishti template matching the package in Canva.
- [ ] **Insert content**: Copy the refined analysis from the LLM output into corresponding layout pages.
- [ ] **Insert graphics**: Add screenshots of competitor ads, PageSpeed dials, and GMB maps.
- [ ] **Assemble scorecards**: Configure comparison matrices and SWOT blocks using our typography and color codes.
- [ ] **Walkthrough recording**: Record a 10-15 minute Loom screen-share video walking the client through the executive summary and key findings.
- [ ] **Build worksheets**: Build clean companion Google Sheets containing keyword sets and content calendars.

---

## 5. CLIENT PRESENTATION (Days 7-10)
- [ ] **Send preview**: Email the client their Loom walkthrough preview link and scheduling options.
- [ ] **Strategy session**: Conduct a 60-120 minute video presentation call.
  - Walk the client through the report findings step-by-step.
  - Focus on the *Quick Wins* and *90-Day Execution Roadmap*.
  - Answer questions and clarify digital marketing terms.
- [ ] **Deliver assets**: Share links to the high-resolution printable PDF report and Google Sheets.

---

## 6. POST-DELIVERY & RETENTION (Days 10-30)
- [ ] **7-day check-in**: Email the client to verify they have accessed the files and ask if their team has any questions.
- [ ] **14-day follow-up**: Check if they have initiated the "Week 1 Quick Wins" checklist.
- [ ] **Testimonial capture**: Request a written review or short video testimonial about the report quality.
- [ ] **Service cross-sell**: Highlight that report clients receive a **20% discount** on any RoasBodhi agency implementation retainers (Meta Ads, Google Ads, Web Development, or GBP SEO).
- [ ] **Check-in cadence**: For Trikaldarshi clients, set up a recurring 30-day strategy consultation checkpoint.
