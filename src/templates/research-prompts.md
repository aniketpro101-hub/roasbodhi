## MASTER RESEARCH PROMPT — CHAKSHU (₹4,999 REPORT)

**Instructions**: Copy the entire block below, fill in the client and competitor variables, paste the raw data harvested from tools (SEMrush, Meta Ad Library, PageSpeed, etc.), and send it to the AI.

```text
You are a senior market research analyst and competitive intelligence expert specializing in Indian local markets.

Create a comprehensive 25-30 page research report for the client below.

CLIENT DETAILS:
Business Name: [CLIENT_BUSINESS]
Industry: [CLIENT_INDUSTRY]
City: [CLIENT_CITY], [STATE]
Website: [CLIENT_URL]
Products/Services: [WHAT_THEY_SELL]
Target Audience: [THEIR_CUSTOMERS]
Monthly Revenue (approx): [REVENUE_RANGE]
Current Marketing: [WHAT_THEY_DO_NOW]
Main Goal: [WHAT_THEY_WANT]

COMPETITORS TO ANALYZE:
1. [COMPETITOR_1_NAME] — [URL]
2. [COMPETITOR_2_NAME] — [URL]
3. [COMPETITOR_3_NAME] — [URL]

RESEARCH DATA I HAVE COLLECTED:
[PASTE DATA FROM TOOLS HERE]
- SEMrush data for their keywords
- Meta Ad Library screenshots/ad copies
- Google Ads Transparency data
- PageSpeed Insights score
- Social media follower and engagement counts
- GMB/GBP profile metrics (reviews, count, average rating)

---

REPORT SECTIONS TO GENERATE:

SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (2 pages)
Write a clear, high-level summary covering:
- Client's current digital position (strengths and gaps)
- Key competitive threats from the 3 competitors
- Top 3 growth opportunities discovered
- Recommended immediate actions
- Expected business impact of recommendations
Include a one-page scorecard table benchmarking the client against Competitors 1, 2, and 3 across core digital vectors. Use professional consultant tone.

SECTION 2: YOUR BUSINESS DIGITAL AUDIT (5 pages)
Analyze the client's current state:
- Website audit (loading speed, mobile friendliness, SEO layout, conversion UX)
- Social media presence (Instagram grid quality, engagement patterns)
- GBP (Google Business Profile) map presence
- Current advertising activity (if any)
- Content quality and messaging assessment
- Overall digital health score (out of 100)
Include specific metrics and honest, constructive feedback.

SECTION 3: COMPETITOR DEEP DIVE (8 pages)
For EACH of the 3 competitors, write a detailed breakdown covering:
- Website quality (design layout, page load speed, mobile UX)
- SEO strategy (organic keyword focus, estimated traffic, backlink profile strength)
- Social media strategy (platforms used, content themes, posting frequencies)
- Ad strategy (creative formats, discount offers, active funnel style)
- GBP map ranking optimization status
- Competitor SWOT breakdown: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities (what client can steal), and Threats
Include a comparative table:
Vector | Client | [COMPETITOR_1] | [COMPETITOR_2] | [COMPETITOR_3]

SECTION 4: LOCAL MARKET INSIGHTS (3 pages)
For [CLIENT_CITY], [STATE]:
- Market demand size for [CLIENT_INDUSTRY]
- Local consumer behavior patterns (search trends, local preferences)
- Best digital marketing channels for this specific city
- Density and intensity of local competition
- Local pricing expectations and price sensitivity levels

SECTION 5: KEYWORD OPPORTUNITIES (3 pages)
Analyze search data and map out:
- Top 30 commercial-intent search keywords for client's business
- Local monthly search volume estimates
- Difficulty/competition level (high, medium, low)
- Priority score (1-10) based on conversion intent
Format as a table:
Keyword | Monthly Volume | Difficulty | Commercial Intent | Priority Score

SECTION 6: QUICK WINS ACTION PLAN (4 pages)
10 immediate, low-cost improvements the client can make:
For each:
- Action description
- Implementation steps
- Business justification
- Impact level (High/Med/Low)
- Time & Cost estimate
- Priority level (1-10)

SECTION 7: GROWTH OPPORTUNITIES (3 pages)
5 high-impact, medium-to-long-term strategies:
For each:
- Strategy description
- Market evidence supporting it
- Execution plan
- Estimated revenue/conversion impact
- Resources required

SECTION 8: NEXT STEPS (2 pages)
A structured execution plan:
- Immediate week-1 actions
- Month-1 tasks
- Quarter-1 milestones
- How RoasBodhi can support execution (soft pitch for retainers)

---

FORMATTING REQUIREMENTS:
- Tone: Professional, authoritative, data-driven.
- Currency: Express financial values in Indian Rupees (₹) and Lakhs/Crores.
- Bold key metrics and critical insights.
- Suggest layout instructions (e.g., "[Insert Pie Chart showing Keyword Share here]") for Canva designers.
- Avoid generic placeholders. Write the full analysis.
```

## MASTER RESEARCH PROMPT — DIVYA DRISHTI (₹14,999 REPORT)

**Instructions**: Copy the entire block below, fill in the client and 5 competitor details, paste your tool research data, and run it. This prompt commands the AI to create a comprehensive 50-60 page deep-dive analysis.

```text
You are a senior principal competitive strategist and digital growth lead specializing in Indian tier-1 and tier-2 business markets.

Create a highly detailed, 50-60 page deep-dive market intelligence report for the client below.

CLIENT DETAILS:
Business Name: [CLIENT_BUSINESS]
Industry: [CLIENT_INDUSTRY]
City: [CLIENT_CITY], [STATE]
Website: [CLIENT_URL]
Products/Services: [WHAT_THEY_SELL]

1. [COMPETITOR_1_NAME] — [URL]
2. [COMPETITOR_2_NAME] — [URL]
3. [COMPETITOR_3_NAME] — [URL]
4. [COMPETITOR_4_NAME] — [URL]
5. [COMPETITOR_5_NAME] — [URL]

RESEARCH DATA HARVESTED:
[PASTE DATA FROM TOOLS HERE]
- Detailed SEMrush/Ubersuggest exports for all 6 websites (Client + 5 Competitors)
- Meta Ads Transparency outputs (active creatives, landing pages, copy variants)
- Google Ads Transparency Center ad logs
- Google PageSpeed insights (Core Web Vitals scores, desktop and mobile)
- Social Blade or platform logs for Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube channels
- Google Business Profile listings (review volumes, photo count, local map ranks)

---

REPORT SECTIONS TO GENERATE:

SECTION 1: EXECUTIVE STRATEGIC BRIEF (3 pages)
- Macro-level view of the competitive matrix.
- Key findings from the competitor reverse-engineering.
- Recommended digital priorities (High, Medium, Low impact).
- Core revenue gaps identified.
- Scorecard table rating the client and 5 competitors across 10 critical digital vectors.

SECTION 2: COMPLETE CLIENT DIGITAL AUDIT (7 pages)
- Website Audit: On-page SEO structure, UX/conversion funnel gaps, mobile layout bottlenecks, PageSpeed Core Web Vitals breakdown.
- Local Visibility: Google Business Profile health check (citations, reviews, optimization score).
- Social footprint: Instagram grid layout, video/reels execution, community engagement metrics.
- Ad accounts evaluation: Analysis of pixel setups, tracking APIs, past campaign performance patterns (if data provided).
- Messaging audit: Brand positioning clarity, USP statement strength, friction points in booking/sales funnel.
- Performance Index Score: Comprehensive scorecard (out of 100).

SECTION 3: 5-COMPETITOR LANDSCAPE ANALYSIS (15 pages)
Analyze EACH of the 5 competitors deeply across:
- Traffic Sources: Organic vs Paid vs Direct vs Social distribution.
- Ad Funnels: Meta and Google ad formats, hooks, copy structures, landing page conversions, and call-to-actions.
- Content Strategy: Organic ranking terms, top blog pages, Reels/social content performance.
- Customer Satisfaction: GBP review analysis, identifying recurring customer complaints (weaknesses to exploit) and praises (strengths to match).
- Competitive Matrix Table comparing: Speed, Domain Authority, Est. Ad Spend, GMB Rating, Citation Count, Social Followers, Funnel Type.

SECTION 4: LOCAL DEMAND & BUYER BEHAVIOR (4 pages)
- Search volume trends for the industry in [CLIENT_CITY] and [STATE].
- Buyer segment demographics, psychographics, and primary buying objections.
- Regional preferences (language, color choice, cultural factors in ads).
- Best-performing marketing channels in this specific geographic cluster.

SECTION 5: SEO DEEP DIVE & KEYWORD MAP (6 pages)
- List of 100 high-intent keywords categorized by user intent (Informational, Navigational, Transactional, Commercial).
- Volume, difficulty score, and estimated cost-per-click (CPC) for each term.
- Competitive Gap Analysis: High-traffic terms your competitors rank for but you do not.
- Technical SEO checklist: Site structure recommendations, schema markup suggestions, indexing fixes.
- Format as a clear table.

SECTION 6: CUSTOMER PERSONAS (4 pages)
Create 3 distinct, highly detailed local buyer personas:
- Persona Name, Age, Occupation, Income Level.
- Primary goals, challenges, and buying friction points.
- Typical search terms they use.
- Content format preferences (e.g. short reels vs deep blog articles).
- Custom marketing pitch for each persona.

SECTION 7: AD STRATEGY & FUNNEL BLUEPRINT (6 pages)
- Meta Ads Funnel: Detailed Campaign-Adsetset-Ad structure (TOF, MOF, BOF targeting rules).
- Google Ads Funnel: Exact keyword match type strategy, ad groups structure, extensions.
- Ad Copy templates: 5 distinct high-converting ad copy angles (Pain-point, Benefit-led, Social Proof, Offer-led, Urgency-led).
- Creative directives: Styling suggestions (formats, graphic templates, reel outlines).

SECTION 8: 30-DAY CONTENT CALENDAR (5 pages)
A complete blueprint for organic channels:
- 30 daily content ideas (15 Instagram Reels, 10 static/carousel posts, 5 blog posts).
- Exact hook ideas, body outlines, and CTA directives for each day.
- Theme matrix (Authority building, Trust signals, Engagement hooks, Sales conversion).

SECTION 9: 90-DAY PRIORITIZED GROWTH ROADMAP (4 pages)
A week-by-week implementation plan divided into 3 phases:
- Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Technical fixes, GBP setup, optimization foundation.
- Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Core funnels buildout, landing pages launch, TOF ad setups.
- Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Audience scaling, retargeting funnels, lead nurture automation.
Include target KPIs for each phase.

SECTION 10: NEXT STEPS & IMPLEMENTATION ASSISTANCE (2 pages)
- Immediate priorities for Week 1.
- Allocation of roles (internal team vs external agency).
- Checklist of resources required.
- How RoasBodhi can execute the plan (full pitch for our service retainers).

---

FORMATTING REQUIREMENTS:
- Tone: Highly strategic, analytical, authoritative, executive-ready.
- Currency: Indian Rupees (₹), lakhs, and crores.
- No brevity shortcuts. Provide full copy examples, keyword sets, and content calendar details.
- Bold key takeaways.
- Include schema layout maps where appropriate.
```

## CANVA TEMPLATE DESIGN SPECIFICATIONS

Use the following design parameters when configuring the Canva template for Bodhi Drishti reports to maintain premium visual aesthetics.

### 1. Typography Rules
- **Headers**: Outfit or Montserrat (Bold/Extra Bold)
- **Body Text**: Inter or Outfit (Regular)
- **Code/Data Elements**: Roboto Mono or Courier Prime

### 2. Palette Usage
- **Deep Indigo (#1E1B4B)**: Primary background for Cover, Section Headers, and Title bars.
- **Cosmic Purple (#7C3AED)**: Primary accent color for bullet icons, card borders, and primary buttons.
- **Saffron (#FF6B2B)**: Primary highlight color for stats callouts, active timeline steps, and badges.
- **Emerald (#059669)**: Mapped for positive data signals, high scores, and success highlights.
- **Red (#EF4444)**: Mapped for warning signals, low speed scores, and negative gap analysis.
- **White (#FFFFFF)**: Primary page background.
- **Charcoal (#374151)**: Primary text color for body paragraphs.
- **Ghost White (#F8F7FF)**: Background shade for callout cards and table alternate rows.

### 3. Layout Grid
- Cover Page: Deep Indigo background with minimal gold lines, large sans-serif typography, client branding stamp, and confidentiality ribbon.
- Header & Footer: Page title and confidentiality level on top. Page numbering and "RoasBodhi™ Systems" logo watermark on the bottom right.
- Scorecards & SWOT: 2x2 grid frames with thin cosmic purple borders. Each sector has a saffron bold title header.
