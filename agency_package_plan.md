## 4. 💡 How to Start (Quick Action Steps)

### Phase 1 — Foundation (Week 1-2)
- [ ] Finalize agency name & register domain
- [ ] Create your own agency website (portfolio + packages + contact)
- [ ] Set up business Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook page
- [ ] Create a **rate card PDF** (professional, branded)
- [ ] Open a current/business bank account
- [ ] Register as a sole proprietorship or LLP (optional initially)

### Phase 2 — Portfolio Building (Week 2-4)
- [ ] Do **2-3 projects for free or at cost** to build portfolio & testimonials
- [ ] Create case studies with screenshots + results
- [ ] Record short video testimonials from early clients

### Phase 3 — Client Acquisition (Ongoing)
- [ ] **Cold outreach** — DM local businesses on Instagram, visit shops
- [ ] **Run your own Meta ads** targeting local business owners (practice + leads!)
- [ ] Join **local business WhatsApp groups** and Facebook groups
- [ ] **Freelancing platforms** — Fiverr, Upwork, Freelancer (for initial cash flow)
- [ ] **Google My Business** — Set up your own GMB and rank locally
- [ ] **Referral program** — Offer ₹1,000–₹2,000 per referral to existing clients

### Phase 4 — Scale (Month 3+)
- [ ] Hire a freelance graphic designer for ad creatives
- [ ] Build SOPs (Standard Operating Procedures) for each service
- [ ] Consider hiring a junior for social media posting work
- [ ] Start content marketing — YouTube, Reels, LinkedIn posts about digital marketing tips

## 7. ⚡ Pro Tips

1. **Always charge setup fees** — Even a ₹2,000-₹5,000 setup fee filters out non-serious clients
2. **3-month minimum contracts** — Especially for ads (results take time to optimize)
3. **Show ROI, not vanity metrics** — Leads, calls, sales > likes, followers
4. **Niche down early** — E.g., "Digital marketing for restaurants in Pune" beats "we do everything for everyone"
5. **Document everything** — Screenshots of results, before/after, analytics = future case studies
6. **Invoice professionally** — Use GST invoicing once you cross ₹20L turnover threshold
